FAQ's

1. How do I earn credits ?

You can earn credits in the following ways :
a) Write an article related to JS or NodeJS. You get 5 credits per post Click here to post an article.
b) Post a KM link related to JS and NodeJS. You get 10 credits per link Click here to add link to KM.
c) Take coding challenge to earn maximum points. You get 30 credits Coming Soon...

2. How to post article ?

You can click on `Create Post` button, which opens a popup window with a text editor. This editor is a markdown editor. Write your article and select the category and post. Once done, you article would reflect in the articles section and your credits automatically added to the credits table.

3. How do I add KM link ?

You can click on `Add KM link` button in dashboard, which gives you a window to add link. You can fill the details and add the KM link, where your content is available.

4. How do I open the Gift Box ?

The minimum number of credits required to open the box is 100. Once you reach the target credits, the box automatically opens.
